ABOUT US

● An Online Art Gallery is a buying/selling platform.
● It provides artists a medium to showcase their talent and to earn by selling their art work.
● Customers using this platform can explore various art forms and can easily buy it.
● This platform will save time of artists as well as of customers by eliminating the need to visit shops/markets.
● A brief description is given with each artwork to let the customers have a complete idea about the art work they are purchasing.
● It also manages the information of Artists, Customers and Payments.
● It is a way through which different art forms can be introduced to the people.
